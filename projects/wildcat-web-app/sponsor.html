Sponsor a Resident

What can you do to help?

You may ask yourself sometimes, "What can I do to help take care of these beautiful animals?" Well, we have an answer for you! You can sponsor one of these cats.

Sponsoring one of our animals helps immensely with the costs associated with providing them with an appropriate diet, living environment, and important medical treatment. When you sponsor one of these cats -- and you get to choose which one! -- you are helping to ensure that they live a long, healthy, and happy life.

If you are interested in sponsoring one of our residents, please fill out the form below so we can contact you. Thank-you!
